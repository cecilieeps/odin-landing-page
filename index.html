Sammies do not descend from wolves...

They are sweet aloof goofy goobers easily mistaken for fluffy clouds (but if you really wanna know, they are wild dogs). They have selective hearing and act as if you don't exist whenever they see another goober a million miles away but we love them nonethesless.
Fluffy Goobers is a social/dog-sitting group where Samoyed owners can bring their fluffy cloud for a social afternoon surrounded by other fluffy clouds.

Unable to find someone to watch your goober during the day? No problem! Register your goober by clicking on the Sign Up button below and drop them off at our headquarters at your chosen time. They'll be pampered so much while you're away you will need to drag them home.
